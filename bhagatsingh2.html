Bhagat Singh

Bhagat Singh was a charismatic Indian revolutionary who participated in a short but influential revolutionary movement against British rule in India. He was a prominent figure in the Indian independence movement.

Key Contributions: Central Assembly Bombing, advocating for socialism, inspiring youth with his sacrifice.
